BREAKING: Claude can now analyse stock market like a senior analyst at Wall Street for free.

Here are 7 ChatGPT prompts to automate your stock marketing trading: 👇

![Image](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HH9aHvRaYAA4yTG?format=jpg&name=large)

---

1\. Full Stock Breakdown

“Act as a senior equity research analyst. Analyze the stock \[insert ticker\] and provide a structured breakdown including business model, revenue drivers, competitive position, recent financial performance, and major risk factors. Present the analysis

---

2\. Financial Statement Deep Dive

“Analyze the latest income statement, balance sheet, and cash flow statement of \[insert company\]. Identify growth trends, profit margins, debt levels, cash generation strength, and financial red flags. Data: \[paste financial data\].”

---

3\. Valuation Assessment

“Evaluate whether \[insert company\] appears undervalued, fairly valued, or overvalued based on earnings, growth rate, and industry comparison. Explain the reasoning clearly without speculation.”

---

4\. Industry & Competitive Analysis

“Analyze the industry in which \[insert company\] operates. Identify key competitors, market trends, growth drivers, and threats that may affect long-term performance.”

---

5\. Risk Scenario Mapping

“List potential downside scenarios for \[insert stock\]. Include macroeconomic risks, industry disruption risks, management risks, and financial risks. Estimate how each could impact performance.”

---

6\. Technical Structure Evaluation

“Analyze the recent price chart of \[insert stock\]. Identify trend direction, support and resistance zones, momentum signals, and possible breakout or breakdown areas. Price data: \[paste\].”

---

7\. Complete Stock Analysis

“Combine business analysis, financial health, valuation, industry context, risk assessment, and technical structure into a structured investment summary for \[insert stock\].”
