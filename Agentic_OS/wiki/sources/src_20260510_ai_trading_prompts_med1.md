## Summary

A collection of 7 structured prompts for using Claude/LLMs as a stock analyst. Covers the full equity research workflow from business breakdown to technical structure. Prompts use `[insert ticker]` / `[paste data]` pattern — fill in and run.

## The 7 Prompts

**1. Full Stock Breakdown**
> "Act as a senior equity research analyst. Analyze the stock [ticker] and provide a structured breakdown including business model, revenue drivers, competitive position, recent financial performance, and major risk factors."

**2. Financial Statement Deep Dive**
> "Analyze the latest income statement, balance sheet, and cash flow statement of [company]. Identify growth trends, profit margins, debt levels, cash generation strength, and financial red flags. Data: [paste financial data]."

**3. Valuation Assessment**
> "Evaluate whether [company] appears undervalued, fairly valued, or overvalued based on earnings, growth rate, and industry comparison. Explain the reasoning clearly without speculation."

**4. Industry & Competitive Analysis**
> "Analyze the industry in which [company] operates. Identify key competitors, market trends, growth drivers, and threats that may affect long-term performance."

**5. Risk Scenario Mapping**
> "List potential downside scenarios for [stock]. Include macroeconomic risks, industry disruption risks, management risks, and financial risks. Estimate how each could impact performance."

**6. Technical Structure Evaluation**
> "Analyze the recent price chart of [stock]. Identify trend direction, support and resistance zones, momentum signals, and possible breakout or breakdown areas. Price data: [paste]."

**7. Complete Stock Analysis**
> "Combine business analysis, financial health, valuation, industry context, risk assessment, and technical structure into a structured investment summary for [stock]."
