# Q2 2026 Goals

Three priorities for [[blaine]] by end of July 2026. If any of these aren't done by then, Q2 was wasted.

## Goal 1 — Fix the P&L

Cut per-trade losses to 0.25–0.33% consistently. This is the most urgent. Every trade that bleeds more than that is compounding the problem. See [[trading_context]] for the full trading setup.

**What "done" looks like:** Average loss per losing trade is 0.25–0.33% of account for a full month.

## Goal 2 — Finish the Trade Journal / backtest prototype

A working system to log trades, tag setups, and review what worked. This feeds directly into Goal 1 — you can't fix what you can't see.

**What "done" looks like:** Blaine is actually logging trades in it after every session and reviewing weekly.

## Goal 3 — Build the TOS strategy backtester

Full system inside thinkorswim with:
- **Dilution Tracker** — flags stocks with share dilution risk
- **Learning System** — turns backtest reviews into lessons, not just logs
- **Thinkscript scan filters** — coded watchlist scans that produce tradeable tickers

**What "done" looks like:** Running a backtest produces a ranked list of tickers that can be traded the next session.

## Priority order

Goal 1 first — it stops the bleeding. Goal 2 second — it makes Goal 1 sustainable. Goal 3 third — it's the product, but only after the foundation is solid.

## Constraint

All of this is [[blaine]] solo, with [[adhd_workflow]] as the dominant operational challenge.
