# How to catch the bottom Bar by bar

How do you catch the bounce after a 25% sell-off without guessing? PLTR dropped hard, six red days in a row, down 25%, but it was one of the strongest names in the market just a week earlier. So, how do you know when the flush is over and the bounce is ready? Here's the blueprint. Multi-day sell-off, price expanding to the downside, still a fundamentally strong stock. Now, zoom in on the 2-minute chart. Wait for this moment. A clean 90 EMA crossover the 21 EMA after it has acted as resistance throughout the morning. That's your intraday signal. The short-term trend is starting to reverse. This isn't guessing, it's structure. Let the EMAs show you when the knife has stopped falling.
