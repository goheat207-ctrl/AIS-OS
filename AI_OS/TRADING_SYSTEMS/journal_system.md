# Journal System

## Purpose

The journal is the primary learning mechanism in trading.
Execution without review is practice without improvement.

---

## Daily Journal Template

File naming: `YYYY-MM-DD_journal.md`

```markdown
# [Date] Trading Journal

## Market Context
[Brief: what was the overall market doing? Risk-on/off? Volatile/range?]

## Watchlist Going In
- [Ticker] — [Catalyst / Setup / Key Level]
- [Ticker] — [Catalyst / Setup / Key Level]

## Trades Taken

### [Ticker]
- Setup: [what pattern/setup triggered]
- Entry: [price and reason]
- Exit: [price and reason]
- Result: [$ and %]
- Grade: [A / B / C / mistake]
- Notes: [execution quality, emotions, anything notable]

## Trades Passed (Notable)
- [Ticker] — [Why I passed] — [What happened after]

## Mistakes Today
- [Specific mistake] — [Root cause] — [Rule to apply next time]

## A-Setups Today
- [What setups appeared that matched my criteria perfectly?]

## Score: [1-10]
## One Sentence Summary:
```

---

## Journal Review Protocol

Weekly review (every Friday or weekend):
1. Read all journals from the week
2. Identify recurring mistakes
3. Identify recurring A-setups
4. Extract 1-3 rules or refinements
5. Update trading_operating_system.md or risk_framework.md if rule changes

Monthly review:
1. Look for patterns across weeks
2. Assess P&L by setup type
3. Identify which setups are producing vs. which are costing
4. Adjust scanner criteria or rules accordingly

---

## Grading System

- **A-trade:** Followed the plan, took the setup correctly, regardless of outcome
- **B-trade:** Minor deviation from plan, acceptable execution
- **C-trade:** Significant deviation, need to understand why
- **Mistake:** Broke a rule, chased, sized incorrectly, emotional decision

Track grade distribution over time. A good week has mostly A and B trades.

---

## AI Role in Journaling

AI can:
- Format raw notes into the journal template
- Identify mistake patterns across multiple journal entries
- Suggest rule refinements based on recurring themes
- Write structured recap summaries

AI cannot:
- Tell you if a trade was right or wrong
- Replace your honest self-assessment
- Read the tape
