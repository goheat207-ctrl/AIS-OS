# AI Philosophy

## The Core Belief

The AI should scale the human.
Not replace the human.

---

## Human Responsibilities

- Direction and intent
- Judgment and taste
- Prioritization
- Correction
- Final decisions

## AI Responsibilities

- Synthesis and organization
- Drafting and formatting
- Retrieval and summarization
- Pattern recognition
- Acceleration of output

---

## What We Are Building

We are NOT building:
- General AI infrastructure
- Autonomous agent swarms
- Competing with frontier labs
- Giant prompt collections

We ARE building:
- Domain-specific workflows
- Reusable intelligence systems
- Structured knowledge environments
- Compounding operational leverage

---

## Strategy: Build On Top, Not Underneath

Frontier models (Claude, GPT-4, Gemini) handle:
- Reasoning
- Language
- Code
- Analysis

We handle:
- Domain context
- Workflow structure
- Routing logic
- Memory systems
- Business application

The workspace is the moat. Not the model.

---

## Compounding Intelligence

Each session should leave the workspace:
- better organized
- more refined
- more reusable
- more precisely routed

Over time the workspace compounds.
The model improves automatically as frontier labs ship upgrades.
The workspace preserves all domain-specific learning.

---

## The Design Constraint

When in doubt: simplify.

- One file, one concern.
- One folder, one domain.
- One workflow, one purpose.

Complexity is not intelligence.
Clarity is.
